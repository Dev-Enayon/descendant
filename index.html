About

Members Of Descendants

We shall Succeed

Over the years, God has blessed us with a church family that has grown, spread, and evolved. Many of us began this journey together—worshiping, serving, and building lasting memories within the four walls we once called home. This year, we are excited to welcome every former and longtime member back for a special Descendants Gathering—a time to reconnect, reflect, and celebrate God’s faithfulness through the generations. This gathering is more than a reunion; it is a moment of revival. A moment to see familiar faces, share testimonies, rekindle friendships, and honor the foundation laid by those who came before us. Whether you were with us in the early days or joined along the way, you are a valuable part of our story—and your presence is deeply cherished. Come with joyful hearts as we worship together, share memories, and embrace what God is still doing among us. Let this be a time to remember where we started, appreciate how far we’ve come, and look ahead with hope for the future.
